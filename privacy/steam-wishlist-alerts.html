Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Extension: Steam Wishlist Alerts

Summary: Steam Wishlist Alerts accesses your public Steam wishlist data solely to check for sales. No personal data is collected, stored on external servers, or shared with third parties.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected or sold
  • No browsing history is tracked
  • No data is sent to our servers (we don't have any)
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No cookies are set by the extension

Steam Data Access

The extension accesses publicly available Steam wishlist and store data to determine whether games on your wishlist are currently on sale. This is done by:

  • Reading your Steam wishlist via Steam's public API
  • Checking current pricing/sale information from Steam's store

This data is processed locally in your browser and is only used to generate sale notifications. Your Steam ID or wishlist data is never sent to any server other than Steam's own APIs.

Data Storage

The extension stores your preferences and cached wishlist/sale data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data is never transmitted to any third party.

Permissions

The extension requests permissions necessary to:

  • Access Steam's website/API to fetch wishlist and pricing data
  • Display notifications when sales are detected
  • Run periodic checks in the background

No permissions are used to collect or transmit personal user data.

Third-Party Services

The extension communicates only with Steam's official website and APIs (store.steampowered.com) to retrieve publicly available pricing and wishlist information. No other third-party services are used.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Significant changes will be communicated through extension updates.
